How to use?

The app can be used in 4 ways:

  • Convert fees that appear on the Alma page.
  • Convert fees from the user fees list.
  • Convert the user’s active balance.
  • Convert a specified amount.

With the first 2 options, the app displays a list of fees (those displayed on the Alma page or the entire list of the user’s active fees).

The user can select the relevant fees (one or all). When ‘convert’ is pressed, the app will calculate the total amount of fees in the institution currency as well as in the target currency.

In addition, it is possible to convert the user’s active balance or even enter a free amount to convert from one currency to another.

Important Notes:

By default, 2 numbers will be displayed after the decimal point. This is configurable by administrator.
